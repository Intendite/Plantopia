Shops


BLG Nursery

BLG Nursery is a brick & mortar plant nursery located in the heartlands of East Punggol. Conveniently located at the exit of Riviera LRT Station, we serve as the neighbourhood's one-stop garden center bringing varieties of plants & gardening materials, and to share the joy & knowledge of plants while being close to home.

Tumbleweed Plants

Tumbleweed is an independent plants retail brand, established in Singapore in 2016. We make stylish indoor plants easy and accessible to all with great looking houseplant varieties, the biggest selection of planters and indoor plant care essentials delivered to your door.

Easi Grow

Easi Grow is a Singapore registered company in offering high-quality automated indoor gardening systems to promote our dreams of growing plants, herbs, vegetables and fruits in our urban environment. All indoors growing systems are available in compact, space-saving which are completely automated self watering and soil-less to minimize pest grown. Many of these units are designed to conserve water and energy and maximize the growth of your indoor garden at all times. These are excellent beginner options for those with busy lifestyles.

A Tilly A Day

A Tilly A Day is a one-stop station providing exotic plants, terrariums, accessories and decorations for your creative gardening needs. We started our retail shop at Waterway Point in 2016 and had recently moved our retail to T-Space 01-21 in January 2022.

House of Plants

Rustic greenhouse at Punggol. We offer a wide selection of trendy indoor plants for your living spaces. Let's Green the World, one plant at a time.

Potta Plantta

Potta Plantta strives to inspire the plant-loving community and help them bring nature into their living spaces. We curate artisanal handcrafted pots and lush healthy plants that would instantly beautify your home or work environment.
