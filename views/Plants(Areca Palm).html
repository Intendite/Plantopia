Wormwood

Basic Description: An attractive plant with medicinal property especially the juice, as it can relieve pain from burns if applied topically

Dos: Maintain a dry condition
Light pruning
Dont's: Overcrowd a Succulent they need plenty of place even if they require little to no food
Water too often, you need to wait for the soil to be completely dry before watering again
